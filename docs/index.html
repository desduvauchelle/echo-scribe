Privacy

Your work stays yours.

No cloud backend. No account. No data sold. Dictation, recordings, transcripts, and project context stay under your control on your Mac.

Quick dictation stays ephemeral

Short voice input is processed locally and can be discarded after transcription. You decide what becomes part of your work memory.

Works fully offline

Models download once, then run locally forever. After setup, Echo Scribe works with no internet connection at all.

No account required

There is no sign-up, no license key, no tracking ID. Nothing is phoned home. Install and it works.

Your data, your folder

Notes live in a SQLite file on your Mac. Export it, back it up, or delete it — full control, always.
