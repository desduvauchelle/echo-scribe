Privacy

Your voice never leaves
your Mac.

No cloud backend. No account. No data sold. Echo Scribe is engineered to keep everything local, always.

Audio stays in RAM

Your mic input is processed in memory and discarded the moment transcription finishes. Nothing is ever written to disk.

Works fully offline

Models download once, then run locally forever. After setup, Echo Scribe works with no internet connection at all.

No account required

There is no sign-up, no license key, no tracking ID. Nothing is phoned home. Install and it works.

Your data, your folder

Notes live in a SQLite file on your Mac. Export it, back it up, or delete it — full control, always.

One command.
Running in 60 seconds.

Open Terminal and paste the line below. No Homebrew required.
